# Publication Triggers

Maps development milestones to recommended publication artifacts.
When a milestone is reached, consider producing the corresponding artifact(s).

## Milestone-to-Artifact Mappings

| # | Milestone | Artifact Type | Primary Audience | Rationale |
|---|-----------|---------------|------------------|-----------|
| 1 | First spec completed and implemented | **Article** | Technical | Demonstrate the spec-driven workflow with a concrete example |
| 2 | Three specs closed with evidence | **Showcase** | Executive | Show measurable progress and methodology effectiveness |
| 3 | Process improvement spec created from a signal | **Article** | Influencer | Highlight the self-improving nature of the framework |
| 4 | End of a development phase or sprint | **Metrics** | Executive | Summarize velocity, quality gates passed, and evidence coverage |
| 5 | Major feature shipped to users | **Deck** | Executive | Present the feature's value proposition and delivery story |
| 6 | Architecture decision record (ADR) created | **Article** | Technical | Explain the decision context, options evaluated, and outcome |
| 7 | Backlog re-scored and re-prioritized | **Metrics** | Technical | Show how priorities shifted and why |
| 8 | Project reaches first stakeholder-ready milestone (PoC complete, first demo, or board presentation) | **Infographic Brief** | All audiences | Synthesize vision, architecture, pillars, and metrics into a narrative-arc brief for visual infographic production. Template: `docs/process-kit/infographic-brief-template.md` |

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Not every milestone requires a publication.** Use judgment — publish when the story adds value.
2. **Combine milestones** into a single artifact when they occur close together.
3. **Record every publication** in [REGISTRY.md](REGISTRY.md) after creation.
4. **Select audience variant** based on the intended reader, not the milestone type.
